What is Saju Palja?

As mentioned in class, Saju Palja 사주팔자, also known as "The Four Pillars of Destiny," or "Ba-Zi" in Chinese, means the four pillars (saju) and eight characters (palja). According to the saju palja concept, a person's destiny or fate can be divined by the two sexagenary cycle characters assigned to their birth year, month, day, and hour. A person's birth year, month, date and time represent each of the four pillars, and each pillar is made up of two characters. One character represents the "heavenly stem" and another represents the "earthly branch." The eight characters add up to render palja that determines fate.
It is relatively common to see people learning the concept of Fatalism with 8 characters in Korea. These days, computers and information technologies are generally used to analyze the fortune of clients by the scientific methodology of statistics. Several popular fortune tellers are relying on YouTube podcast by making an unsolicited analysis of some politicians, businessmen and entertainers.
